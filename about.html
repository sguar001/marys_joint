About Us

We are current UCR students who are trying to jump into the Cannabis industry and shake it up. The lounge will be a sanctuary where people will be able to come and smoke cannabis with others. Currently, in California, there is no place outside one's private residence where cannabis consumption is legally allowed. Accessing the lounge will be easy and we will provide glass for people to use. Got the munchies? There will be an assortment of ice cold beverages that people can order as well as a selection of food items available for purchase. To keep our members entertained we will have several TVs, video game consoles, and great music, with more additions to come.
